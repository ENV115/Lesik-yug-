About Us

“Lesik yug” P O was founded with the support of the Development of Production and Trade Promotion Project, Walnut forest, funded by Seco, ICCO, Hivos and Helvetas. A non-profit organization was officially registered in September 2007. Since 2007 “Bio Service” PF facilitating production, international organic certification, quality management process, training and consultancy. “Bio Service” PF plays a major role in raising awareness and building capacity and promoting a new organic initiatives.

Our mission is protect and develop kyrgyz walnut forest
